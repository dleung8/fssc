Polygon Object

The polygon defines the texture of the ground. A polygon may be associated with a texture or a color. The sides may optionally be dotted with lights. Polygons are primarily used to give the overall appearance of the ground, such as grass, dirt, concrete, or city streets. A polygon, however, does not define a landing area for an airplane. To do this, see the topic on flat areas, and on surface areas.

Polygons are defined by three or more points that can be dragged independently.
